Thepla - a traditional Gujarati (Indian) flatbread

Ingredients: Wheat flour, gram flour, fenugreek leaves, spices (masala: turmeric, red chili, cumin powder, salt), cooking oil and water
Prepare a dough by kneading
Shape the dough into a small ball.
Roll the ball with a rolling pin
Cook on a flat pan using a little oil (thanks to my mom!)
Enjoy with yogurt. Bon appétit!
